Environmental toxicologist working on microplastics, PFAS, regulatory science, and open environmental data tools. 

I develop risk assessment methods and open science tools that help governments, researchers, and communities understand emerging contaminants and make decisions under uncertainty.

I currently serve as a Staff Toxicologist Specialist at the <a href="https://oehha.ca.gov/">California Office of Environmental Health Hazard Assessment (OEHHA)</a>, where I am assessing the human health effects of microplastics in drinking and bottled water to support the implementation of California Senate Bill 1147.

In addition to my duties at OEHHA, I serve as an editor for <a href="https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/cambridge-prisms-plastics">Cambridge Prisms: Plastics</a>, a board member of the <a href="https://mooreplasticresearch.org/">Moore Institute for Plastic Pollution Research</a>, and one of the founders of <a href="https://www.plastiverse.org/">Plastiverse.org</a> — a community hub for plastic pollution research resources.

I am committed to making data and science open to the extent possible, which is why this <a href="https://github.com/ScottCoffin/scottcoffin.github.io">entire website is open-source</a> and may be freely copied by anyone who so wishes.

## Some things I'm known for:

- Helped develop California's [regulatory definition of microplastics](https://www.waterboards.ca.gov/drinking_water/certlic/drinkingwater/docs/stffrprt_jun3.pdf) in drinking water (SB 1422).
- Helped develop California's microplastics [drinking water monitoring](https://www.waterboards.ca.gov/drinking_water/certlic/drinkingwater/docs/2022/mp-hndbk.pdf) and [human health risk assessment frameworks](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/s43591-022-00030-6) — the first in the world.
- Developed health-based and ecological risk frameworks for microplastics used by California's EPA.
- Co-founded [Plastiverse](https://www.plastiverse.org/) as a community hub for plastic pollution research.
- Built or contributed to open data tools including [ToMEx 2.0](https://microplastics.sccwrp.org/), [pSSD++](https://github.com/ScottCoffin/ToMEx2.0_EcoToxRisk/tree/main), and [PFAS data tools](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acs.chemrestox.5c00424).
- [Communicated microplastics and emerging-contaminant science](https://scottcoff.in/Media/) through public talks, media appearances, podcasts, and community-facing events.

Outside of science, I can be found rock climbing, surfing, mountain biking, dancing, playing music, or <a href="https://www.mixcloud.com/scoffincheyenne/">DJing</a>. I'm a Wyoming native living in West Sacramento, California [unceded Patwin Indian territory].
