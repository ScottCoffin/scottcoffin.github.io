# Expertise

## Microplastics risk assessment

I develop and apply risk assessment methods for microplastics in environmental and human health contexts, including drinking water, aquatic ecosystems, exposure assessment, toxicity interpretation, and regulatory decision support. See related [research](/Research/).

## Microplastics in drinking water

My work includes scientific and policy applications related to microplastics monitoring, health-based frameworks, analytical methods, occurrence data, and interpretation of microplastics data for drinking water programs.

## Environmental toxicology and ecotoxicology

My background spans environmental toxicology, ecotoxicology, analytical chemistry, exposure science, and the interpretation of toxicological evidence for environmental contaminants.

## PFAS and toxicokinetics

I work on PFAS toxicokinetics, pharmacokinetic modeling, in vitro-to-in vivo extrapolation, and methods for translating chemical exposure and bioactivity data into risk-relevant metrics.

## Computational toxicology and New Approach Methodologies

My work includes data science, machine learning, toxicokinetic modeling, New Approach Methodologies, and open computational workflows for environmental health assessment. See related [data science and open tools](/Data_Science/).

## Open science and environmental data tools

I build and support open tools and databases that make environmental health information easier to find, interpret, and use, including resources related to [Plastiverse](/Data_Science/#plastiverse), microplastics, PFAS, and risk assessment.

For related public communication, see [media and talks](/Media/). My [CV](/CV.pdf) provides a fuller record of publications, service, and professional experience.
